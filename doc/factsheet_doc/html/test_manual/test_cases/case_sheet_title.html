Case Factsheet Title¶

Purpose: confirm display and edit of Factsheet title.

Setup¶

  1. Step: Start application with Helper Start application

Steps¶

  1. Step: Check window layout.

    1. Expect: The line immediately below the window title contains: (name edit icon), “Unnamed” (Factsheet name), (title edit icon), “New Factsheet” (Factsheet title), (Factsheet menu icon), and (Factsheet Help icon).

  2. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

  3. Step: Edit title to “Untitled”. Do not close popup.

    1. Expect: “Untitled” appears in both Factsheet title and popup.

  4. Step: Press Enter key.

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is “Untitled”.

  5. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

    2. Expect: Title edit popup contains: “Title:”, (apply edit icon), “Untitled” highlighted, and (cancel edit icon).

  6. Step: Click the Factsheet window outside the title edit popup.

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is unchanged (“Untitled”)

  7. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

  8. Step: Press Esc key.

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is unchanged (“Untitled”).

  9. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

  10. Step: Click apply edit icon .

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is unchanged (“Untitled”).

  11. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

  12. Step: Edit title to “<b>Z”. Do not close popup.

    1. Expect: “<b>Z” appears in both Factsheet title and popup.

  13. Step: Edit title to “<b>Z</b>”. Do not close popup.

    1. Expect: “<b>Z</b>” appears in popup.

    2. Expect: “Z” appears in Factsheet title.

  14. Step: Press Enter key.

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is “Z”.

  15. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

  16. Step: Edit title to “<b>Z</b> 1”. Do not close popup.

    1. Expect: “<b>Z</b> 1” appears in popup.

    2. Expect: “Z 1” appears in Factsheet title.

  17. Step: Click apply icon .

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is “Z 1”.

  18. Step: Edit title to “<b>Z</b> 2”. Do not close popup.

    1. Expect: “<b>Z</b> 2” appears in popup.

    2. Expect: “Z 2” appears in Factsheet title.

  19. Step: Click cancel icon .

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is “Z 1”.

  20. Step: Click title edit icon on left of Factsheet title.

    1. Expect: Title edit popup appears below icon.

  21. Step: Edit title to “<b>Z</b> 3”. Do not close popup.

    1. Expect: “<b>Z</b> 3” appears in popup.

    2. Expect: “Z 3” appears in Factsheet title.

  22. Step: Press Esc key.

    1. Expect: The popup disappears.

    2. Expect: Factsheet title is “Z 3”.

  23. Step: Click initial window close icon .

    1. Expect: Data Loss Warning dialog appears.

  24. Step: Click Discard button.

    1. Expect: Dialog and window disappear.

    2. Expect: Application closes.

Teardown¶

  1. Check console for exceptions, GTK errors, and warning messages. There should be none.
